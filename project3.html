Audio Reactive Visuals (No Audio)

What it was supposed to be

A growing collection of audio reactive TouchDesigner visuals that were supposed to respond to original sound. Shapes trembling at bass, lines shivering at high frequencies. A system that listened back.

What exists instead

Visuals that move but aren't listening to anything. They pulse out of habit. They pretend the audio is there.

Why it stalled

The sound never arrived. Each patch sat waiting for a track that didn't exist.

What this fragment reveals

That motion can be muscle memory. That you can build a whole feedback system with no input and it will still try to perform.

Video archive

Clips from different sessions. All built to react to audio. All reacting to nothing now.
