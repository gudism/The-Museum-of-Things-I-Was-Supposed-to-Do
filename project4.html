LA Contracts Bot (Prototype)

A small bot that would scan public contract data from Los Angeles and flag anything that looked strange. Not a full investigation. Just an extra pair of eyes. I imagined graphs, alerts, maybe even a tiny dashboard that would make patterns in the spending a little harder to ignore.
